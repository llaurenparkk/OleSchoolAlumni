Our Mission

The Ole School Alumni Group prides itself on being a visionary, leader, advocate, and catalyst!

The Ole School Alumni Scholarship Group is comprised of James Madison University African American alumni spanning over 40 years. They focus on African Americans to support JMU and is also active in the community both individually and collectively as leaders and advocates for African American higher education and professional success. A contribution to the endowment of the Ole School Alumni Group helps to guarantee continuing support to African Americans at JMU for many years to come, which in turn, helps to open doors of opportunity to many underrepresented students who would otherwise be unable to pursue education at a top tier University.
